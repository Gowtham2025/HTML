Coupe:

A coupe is a two-door car with a sloping roofline, giving it a sporty stance. Most of these coupes come with a powerful engine and feature two-seats or come with smaller rear seats. However, in recent times, coupes are designed with four-doors while its characteristics remain sporty in nature.

Hatchback:

This type of car is a small car with a rear door which opens upwards revealing the boot space to keep your luggage. Excluding the rear door, hatchbacks are built with four-doors, while two-door hatchbacks are also available in the market. Most hatchbacks use the front-wheel drivetrain to propel the vehicle, while performance-oriented hatchbacks can be either a rear-wheel or front-wheel drive.
